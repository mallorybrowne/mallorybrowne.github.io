About

We are on a mission to make the best vegan deserts on the Central Coast.

Bryan and Suzanne have been vegan restauranteurs for 6 years and are thrilled to be taking their talents to the West Coast. After being diagnosed with a dairy intolerance in 2012, Suzanne was determined to make dairy-free into a tasty lifestyle. They have been opening vegan restaurants across the USA with sustainabilty and community as their focus.
